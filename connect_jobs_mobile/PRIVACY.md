# Privacy Policy — Connect Job

Last updated: 2025-10-10

This Privacy Policy explains how Connect Job (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in our mobile applications and website located at https://www.connect-job.com.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information in a transparent and secure manner.

## Information We Collect

- Account information you provide
  - Name, email, password, phone (for company accounts), job title, office name
  - Profile photo and CV/resume files you upload
- Usage information
  - In-app actions related to your profile and job applications (non-identifying)
- Device information
  - Push notification token (FCM/APNs) to deliver notifications to your device
- Cached data (on device)
  - We temporarily cache public job listings on your device to improve performance and offline access

We do NOT collect precise location, contacts, or sensitive device identifiers.

## How We Use Information

- To create and maintain your account
- To enable job search and application features
- To deliver push notifications you opt-in to receive (e.g., new jobs, application status)
- To improve app reliability and user experience (e.g., caching lists locally)

## Data Sharing

- We do NOT sell your personal data.
- We do NOT share your personal data with third-party advertisers.
- We may share data if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

## Data Storage and Security

- In transit: All data is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS).
- At rest (server): Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing; other data is stored on secure servers.
- On device: Job list caches are stored locally using secure app storage (Hive). You can clear cached data using the in-app refresh.

## Data Retention

- We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active, or as required to provide services.
- You may request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal/operational requirements.

## Your Rights

- Access, correction, deletion of your personal data
- Opt-in/opt-out of push notifications in app settings or your device settings

## Children’s Privacy

- Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## International Transfers

- Your data may be processed on servers located outside of your country. We take steps to ensure adequate protection.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact:

- Email: support@connect-job.com
- Website: https://www.connect-job.com

## Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new version with the “Last updated” date.
